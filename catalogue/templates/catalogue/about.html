About Escapism

Escapism is a film camera borrowing platform created for travellers who want to document their journeys through analogue photography. The platform allows users to explore available film cameras, add selected cameras to their Borrow Basket, and submit a request to borrow them for an upcoming trip.

After the trip, users return the camera by post. Selected travel photographs may later be displayed on the website, creating a shared visual archive of journeys, memories, and analogue photography experiences.

Our Concept

Escapism is based on a simple idea: photography should be accessible, creative, and connected to travel. Instead of purchasing a camera, users can borrow one, take it with them on a trip, and experience the process of shooting with film.

The platform focuses on analogue cameras, travel storytelling, and community participation. Each camera includes useful information such as manufacturer, film format, condition, and recommended trip type, helping users choose the right camera for their journey.
