About Escapism

Escapism is a film camera borrowing platform created for travellers who want to experience analogue photography during their trips.

Users can browse available film cameras, add them to a Borrow Basket, and submit a request to borrow them for a journey. The service is free and focuses on travel, creativity, and shared visual storytelling.

After the trip, users return the camera by post. Selected travel photos can later be displayed on the website as part of a shared travel gallery.
