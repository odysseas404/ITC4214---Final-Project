About Escapism

Escapism is a film camera borrowing platform for travellers. Users can browse available analogue cameras, add cameras to their Borrow Basket, and submit a request to borrow them for a trip.

After the trip, users return the camera by post. Selected travel photos can later be displayed on the website as part of a shared travel gallery.
